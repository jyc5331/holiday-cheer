Cookies & Kindness

A helpful site to inspire acts of kindness and holiday cheer all year long

Hello there!

This site is the result of a few friends banding together to make a place where anyone can go for some inspiration to brighten their day. Check out our Cookie Recipe search bar for some reasons to fill up your cookie jar. On the right side of the page you can click “generate” to get an idea for a Random Act of Kindness and keep a log of positivity you may have witnessed out in the world at large. We made this page with the holidays in mind, but we encourage you to incorporate some holiday cheer and good will into your life year round!
